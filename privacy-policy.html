Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Information

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains how Travel by B & T collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us when planning travel.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to plan and manage your travel, including:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number)
  • Traveler details (dates of birth, passport details if required, preferences)
  • Trip preferences (destinations, budgets, special requests)
  • Payment details provided directly to suppliers or through secure links

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Travel planning and research
  • Booking cruises, resorts, flights, and other services
  • Communicating trip details, confirmations, and updates
  • Responding to your questions and support needs

3. How We Share Information

We only share your information with:

  • Travel suppliers (cruise lines, resorts, tour operators, airlines)
  • Gateway Travel, our host agency, when necessary for booking support

We do not sell or share your personal information with outside marketers or unrelated third parties.

4. Payment Information

Travel by B & T does not store or process credit card information directly. All payments are handled securely through supplier payment systems or secure links provided by the vendor.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including secure communication, limited access, and the use of trusted travel suppliers with robust privacy standards.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You may request updates or corrections to your information at any time. You may also request that we delete your information once travel is complete, except where required by law or supplier policy.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website and communication may link to travel suppliers, forms, or external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the revision date.
