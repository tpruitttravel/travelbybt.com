Our Booking Process

Here’s what it looks like to work with Travel by B & T—from first conversation to final travel documents.

Step-by-Step Overview

1

Discovery

We start with a conversation or trip request form. You share your destination ideas, dates or timeframe, who is traveling, and a comfortable budget range.

2

Design & Options

We research and assemble a small set of options that fit your goals. You’ll see clear comparisons— not a confusing spreadsheet of every possible choice.

3

Refine & Confirm

We adjust based on your feedback—swap dates, ships, room types, or itineraries as needed. Once you’re comfortable, we move forward with booking.

4

Booking & Payments

We secure your reservation, walk you through payment timelines, and coordinate with suppliers through our Gateway Travel partnership. You’ll know exactly what’s due and when.

5

Documents & Prep

We provide confirmation details, booking numbers, and relevant instructions, such as online check-in windows, travel document requirements, and key dates.

6

Travel & Support

While you travel, we remain a support resource. If plans change or issues arise, you have a point of contact who knows your trip and your preferences.

Planning Fees & Transparency

Most of the time, our compensation comes from travel suppliers, not by adding hidden fees to your trip. For more complex or time-intensive itineraries, especially multi-city or large group travel, a planning fee may apply.

If a fee is appropriate for your situation, we will clearly outline it before any work begins—so you can decide how you’d like to proceed.

Ready to Start?

If this booking flow aligns with how you like to work, the next step is simple: tell us what you’re thinking about, and we’ll take it from there.
