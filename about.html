About Travel by B & T

Local Advisors. Big-Trip Energy.

Travel by B & T is a Ridgeway, Virginia–based travel agency focused on cruises, all-inclusive resorts, Europe itineraries, and group travel — with real support before, during, and after your trip.

Who We Are

We’re a service-driven travel agency built on trust, responsiveness, and real-world planning experience. You bring us your budget, dates, and goals — we handle the strategy, research, and execution.

As Independent Affiliates of Gateway Travel, we provide national-level buying power while maintaining the flexibility and accessibility of a local advisor relationship.

What We Specialize In

  • Caribbean & Bahamas Cruises
  • All-Inclusive Resorts
  • Europe & Mediterranean Travel
  • Group, Celebration & Custom Itineraries

How We Work

Our process is streamlined, transparent, and built around your priorities:

  • Discovery call to define goals, budget, and dates
  • Targeted research and experience matching
  • Clear comparisons and recommendations
  • Booking, reminders, and post-booking support

No overwhelm. No guesswork. Just clean execution with a dedicated advisor handling the details.
