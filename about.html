About Travel by B & T

Travel by B & T is an independent travel agency and proud Gateway Travel Partner, focused on building stress-free, memorable travel experiences for real-world travelers.

Who We Are

Travel by B & T bridges the gap between “I want to travel” and “I have the time and energy to plan it.”

As an independent affiliate of Gateway Travel, we blend boutique-level service with host-agency relationships, resources, and support—so you get personal attention backed by a serious industry network.

How We Work

  • Your goals and budget guide the entire process.
  • We present curated options, not endless lists.
  • We manage details, deadlines, and documentation.
  • We stay available before, during, and after travel.
