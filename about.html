About Travel by B & T

Travel by B & T is a Ridgeway, Virginia-based travel agency focused on practical, well-planned travel: cruises, all-inclusive resorts, Europe itineraries, and group trips — with real support before, during, and after.

Local Roots, Global Itineraries

We live and work in this community, serving clients from Ridgeway, Martinsville, Henry County, and beyond. When you book with Travel by B & T, you know exactly who you’re working with — not just a call center somewhere with a ticket number.

Our goal is simple: help you pick the right trip at the right time, make the logistics manageable, and be available when you have questions. No hype, no pressure, no hidden agendas.

Independent Affiliate of Gateway Travel

Travel by B & T operates as an Independent Affiliate of Gateway Travel. That relationship gives us access to preferred suppliers, training, and industry support while keeping the service highly personal and local. You get the benefits of a larger network with the accountability of a small business.

How We Work

  • Start with a simple conversation — no pressure, no obligation.
  • Confirm your dates, preferences, budget, and must-haves.
  • Research realistic options that match how you actually travel.
  • Walk you through the trade-offs so you can choose with eyes open.
  • Handle bookings, confirmations, and key reminders.

Advisor Experience

  • Hands-on experience with cruising and all-inclusive travel.
  • Formal supplier training, including Carnival Expert – Diamond Level Graduate and Sandals Elite Advisor Online Training Course (2025).
  • Ongoing supplier and destination training through Gateway Travel.
  • Backgrounds rooted in service, operations, and community leadership.
