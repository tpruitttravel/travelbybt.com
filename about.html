About Travel by B & T

Local Advisors. Big-Trip Energy.

Travel by B & T is a Ridgeway, Virginia–based travel agency focused on cruises, all-inclusive resorts, Europe itineraries, and group travel — with real support before, during, and after your trip.

Who We Are

We’re a small, service-focused team that believes travel planning shouldn’t be stressful. You share your dates, budget, and wish list — we turn that into curated options and handle the logistics behind the scenes.

As Independent Affiliates of Gateway Travel, we work with a wide range of cruise lines, resorts, and tour operators while still giving you the benefit of a local, accessible advisor.

What We Focus On

  • Caribbean and Bahamas cruises
  • All-inclusive resort stays
  • Europe & Mediterranean trips
  • Group and celebration travel

How We Work

We start with a simple conversation — no pressure, no obligation. From there, we:

  • Confirm your dates, preferences, and budget
  • Research and narrow down the right options
  • Walk you through the best fits for your trip
  • Handle bookings, confirmations, and key reminders

You don’t have to spend hours sorting through online reviews and random “deals.” We simplify things and act as your single point of contact.
