About Travel by B & T

Travel by B & T exists to take the friction out of planning and put the focus back on enjoying the journey.

Who We Are

Travel by B & T is an independent travel agency built around real-world schedules and real-life travelers. We know you’re juggling work, family, and community commitments and still want trips that feel intentional, not thrown together.

Our role is to be your behind-the-scenes travel ops team—researching, comparing, and organizing the details so you don’t have to. We bring an operations mindset into every trip: clear expectations, clean timelines, and contingency thinking when it matters.

How We Work

  • We listen first—your preferences drive the plan.
  • We narrow options instead of overwhelming you with links.
  • We communicate clearly at each step of the process.
  • We support you before, during, and after your trip.

Who We’re a Great Fit For

Busy Professionals

You don’t have time to dig through endless options. You want curated, vetted choices and clear next steps.

Couples & Families

From anniversary trips to family cruises, we help you balance budgets, preferences, and logistics.

Groups & Celebrations

Milestone birthdays, friend groups, and reunions where everyone needs to get on the same page.

Our Promise

We won’t promise the cheapest deal on the internet. We will promise that:

  • You’ll understand your options and what’s included.
  • You’ll know who to contact if something changes.
  • Your trip is planned with intention, not guesswork.

If that’s how you want to travel, you’re in the right place.
