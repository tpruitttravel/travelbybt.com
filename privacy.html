Privacy Policy

How Travel by B & T collects, uses, and protects your information as an independent travel agency and Gateway Travel Partner.

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

Travel by B & T (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our travel planning services.

By using our website or submitting information to us, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Travel preferences and interests
  • Travel dates and destinations
  • Passenger details (such as names and birthdates)
  • Loyalty program numbers
  • Passport details and other identification information when required to complete travel bookings

1.2 Technical Data

When you visit our website, we may collect certain technical information automatically, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Device type and operating system

1.3 Booking-Related Information

When you request or book travel through us, we may collect additional information required by travel suppliers, such as airlines, cruise lines, hotels, and tour operators. This may include:

  • Dates of travel
  • Room or cabin preferences
  • Special needs or accessibility requests
  • Emergency contact details

Payment information is typically processed directly by travel suppliers or secure third-party payment processors. We do not store full payment card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide travel consultation and planning services
  • Book travel arrangements with suppliers on your behalf
  • Communicate with you about quotes, confirmations, changes, or issues
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Help prevent fraud and unauthorized activity

We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Share Information

As an independent affiliate of Gateway Travel, we may share your information with Gateway Travel when necessary for booking, support, or compliance purposes. We may also share your information with:

  • Travel suppliers, such as airlines, cruise lines, hotels, tour operators, and transfer companies
  • Payment processors and financial institutions when processing payments
  • Service providers that help us operate our website, email, or other business systems
  • Government authorities, law enforcement, or regulators when required by law

We share only the information reasonably necessary for the recipient to perform their function (for example, a cruise line receiving passenger details to issue tickets).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or storage system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Request access to or correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal and booking-related retention requirements
  • Opt out of marketing or informational emails by using the unsubscribe link or contacting us

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@travelbybt.com.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as cruise lines, resorts, or tour operators). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies directly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental or guardian consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. Any changes are effective when posted unless otherwise stated.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact:

Travel by B & T
Email: info@travelbybt.com
