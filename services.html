Travel Services

We design custom travel experiences with a focus on clarity, convenience, and a little bit of “wow.”

Cruises

Ocean, river, and specialty cruises tailored to your travel style. We help you choose the right cruise line, ship, itinerary, and stateroom so you don’t have to guess.

  • Caribbean, Bahamas, and Mexico itineraries
  • Europe and Mediterranean cruises
  • Alaska and scenic cruising experiences

All-Inclusive Resorts

Resorts where food, drinks, and many activities are bundled into one price. Ideal for travelers who want to relax without thinking about every purchase.

  • Adults-only or family-friendly options
  • Beachfront and pool-focused properties
  • Resorts matched to your vibe and budget

Custom Itineraries

Not every trip fits into a neat package. We build custom itineraries that string together cities, tours, hotels, and transportation into a smooth experience.

  • Pre- and post-cruise hotel nights
  • City stays and rail journeys
  • Multi-stop Europe or domestic trips

Group & Celebration Travel

Milestone birthdays, anniversaries, reunions, and friend trips. We help coordinate the logistics so you can focus on celebrating.

  • Group space on cruises and at resorts
  • Support with invites and communication
  • Centralized planning through one advisor

Planning Fees

In most cases, Travel by B & T is compensated by the travel partners you book with, not by adding hidden charges to your trip. For complex custom itineraries, groups, or requests that require significant research, a planning fee may apply.

If a fee applies to your trip, we will outline it clearly and transparently before any work begins, so you can decide how you’d like to proceed.
