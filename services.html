How Our Services Work

We pair straightforward communication with the backing of our host agency, Gateway Travel, and preferred suppliers. The result: solid options, clear pricing, and one point of contact.

01

Consultation & Trip Design

We start with a short conversation and trip details form. You share dates, budget, and wish-list items; we confirm what’s realistic and map out options.

02

Recommendations & Booking

You’ll receive curated options—not a random list of links. Once you decide, we lock in cabins, rooms, and key components, then provide confirmations and next steps.

03

Support Before & During Travel

We help you understand deadlines, documentation, and expectations. If something changes or questions pop up, you’re not dealing with a call center—you’re working with us.

What Does It Cost to Use a Travel Advisor?

In many cases, our planning services are built into the supplier’s pricing. That means you get professional support at no additional cost to you versus booking direct.

  • No mystery markups—clear communication on any planning fees up front
  • We advocate for you with cruise lines and suppliers when needed
  • You keep your time and peace of mind instead of chasing details
