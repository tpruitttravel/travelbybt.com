How Our Booking Process Works

Simple, personalized, and clear from first message to final boarding pass.

The Three-Step Planning Experience

Whether you’re booking a cruise, an all-inclusive resort, or a custom Europe itinerary, we keep the experience straightforward and stress-free. Here’s exactly what to expect when you work with Travel by B & T.

1

Connect

It starts with a quick connection. You can complete our trip inquiry form, text, email, or message us on Facebook. We’ll ask about your dates, budget, travel style, and any “must-haves” or “absolutely-nots” so we understand what success looks like for you.

This isn’t a sales pitch—it’s a conversation. You’re never locked into booking just because we talk through options.

2

Curate

Using your preferences, we research and build curated recommendations—cruise itineraries, resorts, or custom routes that actually fit what you asked for. No giant list of random links, no pressure.

We’ll walk you through pros and cons, important fine print, payment schedules, and any deadlines so there are no surprises later.

3

Confirm & Go

Once you choose your favorite option, we handle the booking, confirmations, and key details—cabins or rooms, transfers, add-ons, and more. You’ll receive clear documentation and reminders as important dates approach.

If something comes up before or during your trip, you’re not stuck with a call center. You reach out to us, and we help you navigate it.

What to Expect When You Work With Us

Communication

We’ll keep you updated as we research options, place holds, and finalize reservations. You’ll always know what we’re waiting on and what’s coming next.

Transparency

In many cases, our services are built into the supplier pricing, so you’re not paying more than booking direct. If a planning fee applies for complex itineraries, we’ll talk about it up front before any work begins.

Support

From the first conversation until you’re back home, Travel by B & T is your advocate. We leverage our partnership with Gateway Travel and preferred suppliers to help keep things on track—and assist when they don’t go as planned.

Flexibility

Not ready to book yet? No problem. You can start the process early to understand options and pricing, then move forward when the timing is right.
