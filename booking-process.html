How It Works

Our Booking Process

Clear steps from first conversation to final documents — so you always know what’s next and who to contact.

Step 1: Initial Inquiry

You complete our trip inquiry form or reach out directly. We collect basics like dates, destination ideas, budget range, and who’s traveling.

Step 2: Clarify Goals

We review your information and may ask a few follow-up questions so we understand what a “good trip” looks like for you — pace, comfort level, must-haves, and deal-breakers.

Step 3: Research & Shortlist

We research options that align with your criteria and narrow it down to a realistic shortlist. You’re not overwhelmed with dozens of links — you get a manageable set of choices.

Step 4: Review Options

We walk through the options with you — cruise lines, itineraries, resorts, room categories, and approximate pricing. You can ask questions and talk through trade-offs.

Step 5: Confirm & Book

Once you choose an option, we secure the reservation with the supplier, confirm deposits and payment dates, and send you a summary with key terms and deadlines.

Step 6: Pre-Travel Support

Before departure we assist with online check-in, document reminders, and general travel questions — especially for first-time cruisers or resort guests.

During & After Your Trip

We Don’t Disappear After You Book

During Travel

  • Guidance if flights or itineraries change
  • Direction on who to contact for urgent issues
  • Assistance understanding supplier policies in real terms

After You Return

  • Help if post-trip questions or issues come up
  • Notes on what worked well for future planning
  • Building longer-range travel plans if you’d like to look ahead

Planning Fees & Transparency

For most cruise and all-inclusive bookings, there is no separate planning fee — we’re compensated by the supplier. For highly custom, multi-stop itineraries, a planning retainer may apply. If that is the case for your trip, we will discuss it with you upfront before any work begins.
