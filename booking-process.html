How It Works

Our Booking Process

We keep the steps simple, transparent, and low-stress — from first conversation to final documents.

Step 1: Start the Conversation

You submit the trip inquiry form or reach out directly by email or text. We’ll gather the basics: dates, destination ideas, number of travelers, and budget range.

Step 2: Clarify Your Priorities

We ask a few targeted questions to understand what matters most: ship or resort style, pace of travel, excursions vs. downtime, must-see locations, and any accessibility or dietary needs.

Step 3: Research & Curate Options

We research cruise lines, itineraries, resorts, and packages that align with your preferences. Instead of sending you a giant list, we narrow it down to realistic, well-matched options.

Step 4: Review & Refine

We share options with clear explanations of what’s included, what’s not, and key pros/cons. You review, ask questions, and we adjust as needed until we land on the right fit.

Step 5: Confirm & Book

Once you’re ready, we confirm traveler details and move forward with booking. Payments are made directly to the supplier through secure systems — we’ll walk you through each step.

Step 6: Documents & Prep

We send confirmations, review key details with you, and provide reminders for:

  • Final payment deadlines
  • Check-in instructions
  • Passport and ID requirements
  • Any pre-trip forms the supplier requires

Step 7: Support While You Travel

If issues arise before or during your trip, you can reach out to us. We’ll help you understand your options and work with the supplier where possible.

Step 8: Post-Trip Follow-Up

Once you’re back, we’re still here — for questions, feedback, or to start planning the next vacation.
