Travel Insurance

Protecting Your Trip & Peace of Mind

Travel insurance helps safeguard your investment against unexpected situations — before and during your trip.

Why Travel Insurance Matters

Travel plans can change for reasons far beyond your control. Travel insurance helps protect you from financial loss and provides support when disruptions occur.

Coverage Often Includes:

  • Trip cancellation or interruption
  • Medical emergencies while traveling
  • Lost, delayed, or damaged luggage
  • Emergency evacuations
  • Travel delays or missed connections
  • Cruise-specific protections

Cancel For Any Reason (CFAR)

Some policies include CFAR upgrade options for additional flexibility, allowing cancellation for personal reasons not covered by standard plans. Availability varies by state and provider.

When Should I Purchase Coverage?

Ideally within 10–21 days of your first payment. This ensures access to the most robust benefits and exclusions.
