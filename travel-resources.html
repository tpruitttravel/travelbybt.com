Cruise Travel Basics

  • Arrive at the port early — usually at least 2 hours before departure.
  • Keep passports, boarding passes, medications, and valuables in your carry-on.
  • Complete online check-in with your cruise line before arrival when available.

Packing Basics

  • Important meds, documents, and one change of clothes in your carry-on.
  • Layers for cooler ship interiors and warmer outdoor decks or ports.
  • Chargers, travel adapters (for international trips), and a small day bag.
