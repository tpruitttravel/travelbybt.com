Cruise Travel Tips

  • Arrive at the port early — usually at least 2 hours before departure.
  • Keep passports, boarding passes, and medications in your carry-on.
  • Review your cruise line’s online check-in process before travel.

Packing Basics

  • Important meds, documents, and one change of clothes in your carry-on.
  • Weather-appropriate layers for both ship and shore.
  • Chargers, travel adapters (for international trips), and a small day bag.
